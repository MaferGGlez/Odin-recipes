Stuffing

Description

This classic Thanksgiving stuffing is packed with savory flavors and has a perfect balance of crunchy and soft textures. Made with bread, vegetables, and herbs, it’s the ideal side dish for your holiday table.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of day-old bread, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Fresh sage, thyme, and parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and celery in butter until soft.
  3. In a large bowl, combine bread cubes, sautéed vegetables, and herbs.
  4. Add broth and toss to coat.
  5. Transfer to a baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes.

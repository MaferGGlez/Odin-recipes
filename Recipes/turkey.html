Thanksgiving Turkey

Description

Roast turkey is the centerpiece of a Thanksgiving meal, bringing family and friends together to enjoy its savory flavor and crispy golden skin. This recipe ensures a tender, juicy turkey that’s full of flavor, perfect for celebrating the holiday.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole turkey (12-14 pounds)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, sage)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Season the turkey inside and out with salt and pepper.
  3. Stuff the cavity with onion, lemon, and fresh herbs.
  4. Brush melted butter over the skin.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven until internal temperature reaches 165°F (about 3 hours).
  6. Let rest for 20 minutes before carving.
